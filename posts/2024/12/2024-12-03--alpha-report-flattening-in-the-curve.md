This report will lay out the TYPES of yield curve moves we have seen over the past 6 trading days and WHY understanding it is an important signal for interest rates and all risk assets this week.

Fundamentally, there are four main TYPES of moves in the yield curve.

*   **Bull Flattening**: The yield curve flattens as long-term rates decrease more than short-term rates.
    
*   **Bear Flattening**: The yield curve flattens as short-term rates increase more than long-term rates.
    
*   **Bull Steepening**: The yield curve steepens as short-term rates decrease more than long-term rates.
    
*   **Bear Steepening**: The yield curve steepens as long-term rates increase more than short-term rates.
