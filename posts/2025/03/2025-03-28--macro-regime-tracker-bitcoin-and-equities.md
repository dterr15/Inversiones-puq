One of the major themes I have laid out for paid subscribers is that macro volatility is NOT over. Notice that 30D volatility remains elevated as 10D volatility is falling marginally. This means on a 30D basis, the drivers in equities to the downside have been significant enough to push equities outside of their historical range. The short-term capitulation has subsided as equities bounced but the bounce has been incredibly weak and macro factors arent shifting enough to justify a shift back to all time highs and low volatility.
