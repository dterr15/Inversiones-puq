Main Ideas:

*   The short-end plumbing is NOT impacting broad beta flows
    
*   We are seeing dispersion and rotation as opposed to a macro inflection point
    
*   The unrealistic expectations of Bitcoin’s returns stem from narratives as opposed to a rigorous analysis of the price action and macro flows
    
*   The signals for WHEN Bitcoin will rally and outperform can only be derived when viewing it in its place on the risk curve relative to equity beta.
